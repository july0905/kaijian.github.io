Haoyuan Zhang

I am a 3rd-year Computer Science Ph.D. Candidate in Risk & Information Management Group, Queen Mary University of London (QMUL).

My supervisors are Dr. William Marsh, Prof. Norman Fenton, and Prof. Martin Neil.

 

Research Interests

My current research topic is using Bayesian network models to improve maintenance decision making from sparse data and expert judgement.

I work on:
  • Hierarchical Bayesian Models
  • Probabilistic Relational Models
  • Heuristic-based Algorithms
With application on:
  • Deterioration Modelling, System Safety and Reliability
  • Railway Maintenance Decision Making
  • Scheduling and Optimization Problems
